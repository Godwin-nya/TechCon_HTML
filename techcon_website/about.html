About TechCon

TechCon began as a small meetup for local developers and has grown into an international conference focused on practical innovation and collaboration. Our mission: to connect builders, share knowledge, and accelerate real-world technology adoption.

Mission

To provide a hands-on, inclusive event where technologists learn from practitioners and leave with actionable skills.

Notable Speakers

  • Dr. Amina Sarkar — AI & Ethics
  • Marcus Lee — Cloud Native Architectures
  • Sanae Rodriguez — Product Leadership

History

Since its founding in 2016, TechCon has hosted workshops, hackathons, and talks across multiple continents.
